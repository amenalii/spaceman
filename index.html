Spaceman

How to Play:

  • Your objective is to guess the space-themed hidden word.
  • Each letter in the word is hidden and must be guessed individually.
  • Select a letter using the onscreen keyboard.
  • Correct guesses will reveal the letter; wrong guesses reduce your attempts.
  • Win by guessing the word before running out of attempts.
  • Click "Restart Game" to play again.
